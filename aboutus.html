About Lamborghini

Founded in 1963, Automobili Lamborghini is headquartered in Sant'Agata Bolognese, in Northern Italy. Lamborghini's story is one of passion, innovation, and speed. From the iconic Miura to the raging Aventador, every model carries the spirit of Italian craftsmanship and bold performance.

Our mission is to challenge the status quo and push the boundaries of automotive engineering. With advanced aerodynamics, cutting-edge technology, and unmistakable design, we create dreams on wheels.
